Who am I?

Hola! My name is Steven Hong and I was born and raised from the Washington metropolitan area. I moved to San Diego in August of 2016 after deciding it was time for a change. "Why San Diego?" many have asked. Well, my best friend who was in the Navy at the time was stationed in Coronado and invited me to visit and the rest was history. I took advantage of having a place to stay while settling to life in San Diego and here I am!
